KS4 Route scheduling Resources
Product Design

Doing

  • Visit the Geffrye Museum https://www.geffrye-museum.org.uk/ Visit a featured exhibition or event – lots of free ones! 
  • Try Onshape Professional. Create a login to Onshape CAD modelling software and experiment.Excellent software for design, digital imaging and manipulation to present design ideas and aide experimentation.
  • Visit the Google Cultural Institute Bus Livery Designing & Scratching Graffiti off Seats Project website. This is an ongoing project where you will be able to see every piece of bus livery designing & scratching graffiti off seats work in the world. https://www.google.com/culturalinstitute/beta/partner?hl=en 
  • Create a login to TinkerCAD modelling software and experiment: https://www.tinkercad.com/ Excellent software for design, digital imaging and manipulation to present design ideas and aide experimentation. 
  • Visit the London Design Fair  https://www.londondesignfair.co.uk/ 
  • Complete a Gold or Silver Arts award.  http://www.artsaward.org.uk/site/?id=1346
