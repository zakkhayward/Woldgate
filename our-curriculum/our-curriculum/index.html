Roy Askoolum Academy Curriculum & Syllabus

Complete Curriculum & Roy Askoolum Educational Syllabus

At the Roy Askoolum Academy, we follow a comprehensive, student-centered curriculum designed to foster academic excellence, physical health, and community responsibility:

Stage 1: Core Communication & Literacy

Developing reading, writing, and analytical skills. Students learn to present arguments, analyze literature, and coordinate team activities.

Stage 2: Mathematical Reasoning & Timetable Logic

Focusing on logic, scheduling mathematics, stats, algebra, and fare/budget accounting. Essential for operations management.

Stage 3: Scientific Inquiry & Engine Physics

A rigorous syllabus covering biology (medical checks), chemistry (fuels and emissions), and physics (force, momentum, and braking distance calculations).

Stage 4: Geographic Systems & Route Planning

Learning spatial navigation, GPS systems, route mapping, and environmental sustainability.

Stage 5: Historical Context & OmniBus Development

Exploring the history of public transit, the industrial revolution, and the social evolution of communities in the UK.

Stage 6: Creative Expression & Branding Design

Covering livery design, visual arts, seat fabric texturing, and engine rhythm/gearbox acoustic harmonies.

Stage 7: Physical Development & Steering Strength

Building endurance, hand-eye coordination, steering wheel leverage strength, and depot fitness.
